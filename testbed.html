Layered Control Design and Hardware-in-the-Loop Testbed Construction.

Constructed a real-time hardware-in-the-loop simulation for wind farms in order to enable testing of cyber attack detection mechanism. The flow field is computed by two python frameworks that are modified to work with external simulation of the turbines. The wakes introduce a coupling between the turbines and the turbines benefit from a higher frequency local controller to counteract the wakes, therefore the a layered control is developed to meet external power needs while minimizing loads on the turbines.
