Switched Control

The heating system is modeled by the dynamics of the boiler, radiator and room temperature, and changes with respect to how much the window is open. The window is actuated by a hydraulic arm. Both heating and the hydraulics are controlled by switched control, therefore the system inhibits switched system dynamics, and the dynamics and controller is described by an automaton.
